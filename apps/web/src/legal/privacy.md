# UDCSP — Privacy notice (GDPR Art. 13/14)

> Citizen-facing privacy notice. This is the source of truth that the web
> portal renders at `/legal/privacy`, the mobile app surfaces in
> Settings → Privacy, and the voice channel reads (TTS) on the second
> "How do you handle my information?" turn.
>
> **Reading note.** This is the readable form. Plain language, no legal

## Who we are

UDCSP — the Unified Digital Citizen Services Platform — is operated on
behalf of three national authorities:

* **Denmark** — Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Udenrigsministeriet)
* **Sweden** — Swedish Migration Agency (Migrationsverket)
* **Norway** — Directorate of Immigration (UDI)

Each authority is the **controller** of your data under the law of their
country. The platform itself is the **processor**.

## What data we collect

* **Identity** — verified by your national digital identity provider
  (MitID in Denmark, BankID in Sweden, Vipps in Norway). We keep the
  proof of identity, not your raw national ID number.
* **What you tell us** — answers to forms, attachments you upload, the
  text and voice of conversations with our citizen-assistant agent.
* **Technical data** — device type, language, the audit trail required
  to keep the service secure.

## Why we use it

* To process your application or enquiry (statutory public task).
* To prove that we treated you fairly (audit, accessibility, AI-Act
  records).
* To notify you of decisions and follow-up actions.

We never sell your data and we never use it to train large language
models.

## Where your data is stored

In your country's primary EU data region — and **nowhere else**:

| If you live in… | Your data is stored in… |
|---|---|
| Denmark | North Europe (Dublin) |
| Sweden  | Sweden Central (Gävle) |
| Norway  | Norway East (Oslo)     |

## Who else sees it

A short, current list lives in [`governance/gdpr/sub-processors.md`](../../../../governance/gdpr/sub-processors.md).
We give you 30 days notice before we add anyone new.

## How long we keep it

| Category | Retention | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Conversation drafts | 30 days | Avoid losing your work; minimised by GDPR Art. 5(1)(c) |
| Submitted application | Until your case is decided + appeal window | Necessary for the service |
| Decision letter | 7–10 years | Required by national archive law, then transferred to the National Archive |
| Audit log | 180 days online, then 7 years archive | Security & accountability (NIS2 Art. 21) |

## Your rights

You can — at any time, free of charge — request:

* A copy of your data (Art. 15)
* Correction of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
* Deletion (Art. 17), subject to retention requirements
* Restriction or objection (Art. 18, 21)
* Portability (Art. 20)
* Lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority

## How to reach us

* **Web portal** — Help → Contact the DPO
* **Email** — `dpo@udcsp.eu`
* **National DPAs** — Datatilsynet (DK · NO), IMY (SE)

## What we ask of you

Please **do not** share your password or your MitID/BankID/Vipps
credentials with anyone. We will never ask for them by email or SMS. If
you suspect a breach, contact `dpo@udcsp.eu`.
