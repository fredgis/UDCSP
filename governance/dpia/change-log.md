# DPIA Change Log

> **Purpose** — chronological journal of every material change that affects
> a DPIA in scope of UDCSP (sub-processor change, model update, lesson
> learnt from a breach, regulatory clarification, etc.). Referenced by
> [`governance/gdpr/sub-processors.md`](../gdpr/sub-processors.md) and
> [`governance/security/breach-notification.md`](../security/breach-notification.md).

| Date | Author | Trigger | Affected DPIA(s) | Action taken | Re-review needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| _yyyy-mm-dd_ | _DPO_ | _e.g. Microsoft Trust Portal sub-processor diff_ | _e.g. dpia-eligibility-model.md_ | _summary_ | _yes/no + due date_ |

## How to add an entry

1. Append a row above (most recent first **after** this header — keep the table
   reverse-chronological so the latest entry is on top).
2. Cross-link the affected DPIA(s) under `governance/dpia/`.
3. If the change closes an open audit / breach action item, link the
   originating ticket or post-mortem under `governance/security/postmortems/`.
4. If the change requires a re-issued sub-processor notice, also append the
   diff to [`governance/gdpr/sub-processor-change-log.md`](../gdpr/sub-processor-change-log.md).

## Retention

Entries are retained for the lifetime of the DPIA they refer to **plus 6
years** (per the broader UDCSP records-retention policy). Pruning earlier
than that requires DPO sign-off.
