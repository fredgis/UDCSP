# AI Act Conformity Assessment Procedure

1. Product owner creates or updates a registry YAML entry before model promotion.
2. A6 attaches Foundry evaluation, tracing, safety and model-card evidence.
3. A13 verifies risk level, lawful basis, data sources, fairness metrics and human oversight.
4. High-risk entries require legal/DPO approval and signed conformity evidence before production.
5. Approved entries are published to Purview AI asset registry and linked to release notes.
